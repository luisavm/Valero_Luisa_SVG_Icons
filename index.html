DOES YOUR CAT HAVE SOME ADORABLE OR ODD BODY LANGUAGE?

Let's do together all the possible cat behaviors to help you find a common language with cats and communicate with them better.

RELAXED

Your cat rubs its head or side, it is just saying hello or relaxed.

PLAYFUL

The tail stick straight up and back bent that your cat wants to play.

HAPPINESS

Raised upward and curled at the end that means your cat is happy and fulfilled after eating food.

GLAD

The tail vibrates slightly that means your cat is glad to see you.

COMFORTABLE

A slow blink indicates that your flurry friend is calm, and feels comfortable and safe.

WORRIED

Keeps knocking its tail on the floor that means your cat is in worried, pain or stressed.

BORED

No interest in activities or sleeping more than usual that means your cat is bored or depressed.
